### [A FAST Survey of H I Absorption in Low-power Radio Sources](https://arxiv.org/abs/2603.05477)

**Authors:** Yang Su, Qingzheng Yu, Taotao Fang, Junfeng Wang, Jianfeng Wu, Bo Zhang
**Relevance Score:** 7.0/10

**Main Question:** The study investigates the interplay between active galactic nuclei (AGN) and their surrounding interstellar medium in low-power radio sources by examining the origin and kinematics of neutral hydrogen (HI) absorbing gas.
